Course assessment

Students will be assessed on the basis of the following components:

  • [30%] Drafting of a one-page summary of each session in the first part of the course. Summaries are to be handed in (physicaly or electronically) by the following session at the latest;

  • [30%] The presentation of a research paper in the second part of the course. Papers will be presented in small groups (2-4 people, depending on the number of attendants);

  • [40%] Drafting of a research report-like assignment focusing on one of the studies presented in class in the second part of the course.

A template for the session summary can be found here.

A reference document with the questions for the report assignment can be found here.

The course instructor will upload a one-page summary of each session by the beginning of the following session.
